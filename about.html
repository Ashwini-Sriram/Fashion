Our planet doesn't have enough natural resources for us to keep doing what we've been doing. Period.

intensive. By investing in new technological solutions, we aim to change the way we make, use
and say goodbye to clothes. No more tattered jeans in household trash and no more heavy reliance
on water-intense fabrics. Instead, we want to eliminate waste and let material flow in circles.
